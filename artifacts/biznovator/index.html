About

What is Biznovator?

Biznovator is a flagship startup innovation competition under SPROUT IT 2025, where students transform bold ideas into scalable ventures through rigorous multi-round evaluation — culminating in a live investor pitch before industry leaders.

🏛️

Hosted by SCIT Pune

Symbiosis Centre for Information Technology — one of India's premier tech institutes — brings you this world-class startup platform.

🎓

Open for UG & PG

Any full-time undergraduate or postgraduate student from a recognized institution can register and compete.

🤝

Cross-College Teams

Teams of 1–5 members from different colleges are welcome. Collaboration across institutions is celebrated.

🏆

Offline Grand Finale

The final round takes place offline at SCIT Pune campus — a true startup summit with real investors in the room.
